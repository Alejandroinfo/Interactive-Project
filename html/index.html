About This Page

The similarity score is built by combining:

  • Mechanics: Rules and systems of play (e.g., deck-building, worker placement).
  • Themes: Narrative or setting (e.g., fantasy, sci-fi, economy).
  • Other variables: Rating, Description, Categories.

Games with more overlap in these areas receive a higher similarity score, which is used to draw links in the graph and rank neighbor games.
